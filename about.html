About AutoGear Hub

This is the official website of AutoGear Hub — a growing automotive parts and second-hand car dealer in Malaysia. We proudly provide 100% original engine parts and quality second-hand cars to our valued customers.

While delivery is not yet available, we offer a convenient Pre-Order and WhatsApp Pickup System. Items can be pre-ordered online and collected in-store. “Buy Now” and “Home Delivery” features will be added soon!

Visit our stores in Kuala Lumpur, Johor Bahru, and Penang — or pre-order directly through the website. Thank you for choosing AutoGear Hub!
